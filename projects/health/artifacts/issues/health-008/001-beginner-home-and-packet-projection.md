# Beginner home and packet projection

Type: AFK

## What to build

Create the smallest complete browser slice: a calm, responsive beginner home
that explains PhilHealth, HMOs, and private insurance, presents the safe jobs
the product can do, and renders the existing `beginner-employer-hmo` packet as
a progressive, source-aware result. Use real product copy and fixture data; do
not expose an empty chat, internal skill taxonomy, or generic dashboard.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] React, Vite, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS build inside `ui/`.
- [ ] A public-interface test fails first, then proves the beginner home and packet projection.
- [ ] The page has one clear guided start, browsable basics, a capability map, and visible safety boundaries.
- [ ] The packet shows summary, freshness, confidence boundary, evidence gaps, human reviewers, and one next step.
- [ ] The layout works at desktop and narrow widths without horizontal overflow.

## User stories covered

US-01 through US-12, US-18, US-22, US-26, US-31.
