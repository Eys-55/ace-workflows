Type: AFK

## What to build

desktop, narrow, touch, high zoom, keyboard-only, and reduced-motion settings.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Browser tests prove the complete guided-start-to-packet path through the real companion.
- [ ] Automated accessibility checks pass and manual evidence covers focus order, visible focus, status announcements, labels, contrast, touch targets, zoom/reflow, and reduced motion.
- [ ] Empty, partial, failure, refusal, permission, stale, offline, unavailable, retry, recovery, and handoff states remain legible and actionable.
- [ ] Screenshots and traces use representative Health content and contain no console errors, failed requests, broken assets, or viewport overflow.
- [ ] Visual critique confirms a beginner learning hub, not a generic card mosaic, admin shell, or renamed workflow dashboard.
