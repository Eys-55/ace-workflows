## Draft

I think in the Philippines, one of the problems we have with health insurance is that we don't even know where to start.

Like, what insurances actually exist? How do you search for them? How do you compare them beyond just asking around?

That became the first real-world workflow I wanted to build while learning harness engineering.

I decided not to build it as a normal web app. I wanted it to feel more like a workflow package you can install into your coding CLI, like Codex or Claude Code, and then work through with an agent.

The workflow helps you navigate different health insurance options in the Philippines, not just PhilHealth. It collects research, compares options, asks questions about your situation, and helps clarify what you should look into before buying.

I'll keep improving this over the next few weeks, and I'll also start publishing more of the workflows I've been building plus what I'm learning about harness engineering along the way.
