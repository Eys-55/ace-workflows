## What to build

Add the interface-direction and states-and-actions references, then carry the
Health, evidence-auditor, revamp, and action-lifecycle scenarios through the
same fresh-agent seam. The skill must turn real workflow content into a visual
thesis, content plan, interaction thesis, domain-specific architecture, and a
complete action-state model before target-project implementation.

The slice is complete only when two domains produce visibly and structurally
different products while sharing accessibility and interaction quality floors.

- [x] Idle, confirmation, running, progress, partial, success, failure, cancel, retry, recovery, empty, refusal, permission, stale, offline, and unavailable states are covered.
- [x] Keyboard, focus, touch target, contrast, responsive, and reduced-motion behavior is required.
- [x] Health and evidence-auditor post-skill outputs have materially different IA, labels, and interaction models.
- [x] Root workflow-state validation and tracker verification pass.
